Our Story & Purpose

Code Her Digital Confidence was founded on the belief that digital skills are the key to economic independence and self-assurance in the 21st century. We saw a gap where women and girls often lacked access to practical, confidence-building technical education.

Our holistic approach combines robust technical training (from basic packages to cloud fundamentals) with essential life and confidence skills (growth mindset, Self-Awareness, Self-Esteem). This ensures every graduate leaves not just skilled, but truly empowered to lead and succeed.

Our Comprehensive Program: Skills for Success

We offer one powerful, holistic program that strategically integrates technical expertise with essential life and confidence skills.

Module 1: Digital Literacy & Technical Mastery

Master the essential technical tools of the modern workplace and navigate the digital landscape with expertise. This module lays your foundation in the digital world.

  • Main Computer Packages (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
  • Digital Communication & Collaboration Tools
  • Internet Safety and Cybersecurity Basics
  • Basic Data Organization & Management

Module 2: Life & Confidence Skills for Leadership

Build the inner foundation—the critical soft skills for career growth, personal leadership, and thriving in any environment. These skills are embedded throughout the technical training.

  • Self-Awareness & Confidence Building
  • Effective Communication & Presentation Skills
  • Growth Mindset & Resilience
  • Goal Setting & Strategic Planning
  • Professional Etiquette & Networking
  • Time Management & Productivity
  • Emotional Intelligence & Empathy
